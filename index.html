Quick start

In this section, I will guide you step-by-step on how to send a weekly newsletter.

Step 1:

Open the Brevo website using the credentials you received from the IT department.

Step 2:

To open the Campaigns page, navigate to the left menu and click on the "Campaigns" option. Once you've reached the Campaigns page, you will find a "Create a Campaign" button located in the top right corner. Click on that button to proceed with creating a new campaign.

Step 3:

Select the type of campaign you want to create, for this case, choose the "Email" option.

Step 4:

Please enter a campaign name such as "ES Library Weekly Newsletter - [date]" for identification and organization purposes.

Step 5:

the Email Settings page to configure your email preferences and settings.

Step 6:

If you want to change the sender email, choose the "Edit From" option to modify the sender information. Select the desired email address and provide the sender name you wish to use for the campaign on the "Edit From" section.

Step 7:

Choose the recipients for the campaign by selecting the "Library Newsletter" list from the available current lists.

Step 8:

Write a subject line for your email and a small description for the current newsletter. You can leave the preview text empty.

Step 9:

From the 'Start designing' options, select 'My Template', and when you find the 'ES Library - Weekly Newsletter', click on 'Use Template'.

Step 10:

Change the images and titles in the template, and please ensure that you have correctly added all the post links.

Step 11:

Click on 'Preview & Test' to send a test email to yourself and your team manager before sending the email to the entire mailing list. Please note that if you couldn't find your email in the test email list, you can simply add it.

Step 12:

Finally, click on 'Schedule' to send the email to the entire list. You can either send it immediately or select a specific time and date for delivery.
